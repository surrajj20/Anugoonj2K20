About us

Anugoonj, which translates to “Echoing of small efforts”, is the monumental annual cultural festival of GGSIPU which takes place in February. Captivating all creative thoroughbreds from more than 100 affiliated colleges, the fest spans three days. This extravaganza is escapism from the laborious college life. Anugoonj is the place to be, whether you want to flaunt your skills, stand with your mouth agape watching the talent, or just use it as an excuse to come and have tons of fun.
